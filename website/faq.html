Frequently Asked Questions

Booking

How can I book?

We use trusted platforms like Airbnb and Vrbo to ensure security and support for both guests and hosts. Click "Book Now" to select your platform. The minimum reservation period is 2 nights.

Are there any restrictions on who can stay?

Minors are only permitted to stay with their parents or legal guardians, and all necessary documentation must be provided before check-in. Pets are prohibited.

Are you a hotel or a vacation rental?

We are a vacation rental offering the entire unit for your stay. While we do not provide daily cleaning or typical hotel services, we supply initial essentials, including towels. Additional cleaning services are available upon request.

What are your rental policy?

Our rental requirements include a signed rental contract, guardian documentation for minors, valid identification must be provided for all guests prior to check-in, and only registered guests are allowed in the unit.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full 100% refund for cancellations made 5 days before check-in.

Building & Apartment Rules

What are the building rules?

No parties or loud gatherings: Quiet hours are from 10 PM to 8 AM. No smoking inside the unit or building. Pets are prohibited. Only registered guests are allowed. Supervising children at all times is required in pool areas.

What is the check-in and check-out process?

Standard check-in time is 3 PM, and check-out is at 11 AM. Identification and required paperwork must be submitted before arrival. Wristbands will be provided for building access.

What amenities are available in the unit?

Amenities include high-speed Wi-Fi, 2 smart TVs with Netflix and other streaming apps, a fully equipped kitchen, a private jacuzzi, air conditioning, board games, and a playard for babies.

What is the guest capacity of the unit?

The suite can comfortably accommodate up to 5 guests with the following sleeping arrangements: 1 Queen Bed in the main bedroom, 1 Small Sofa Bed for a child, 1 Queen Sofa Bed in the living room, and 1 Playard for a baby.

Is the apartment child-friendly and safe?

Yes, the suite includes a playard for babies and child-friendly amenities, such as board games. The apartment is located on the 17th floor, so parents or guardians are responsible for the well-being of their children at all times.

What is provided in terms of linens and towels?

We provide fresh linens for all beds and at least 1 body towel and 1 pool/beach towel per guest.

Is there a laundry service available?

There is no on-site laundry service, but a local company offers pick-up and delivery laundry service. Details can be found in the apartment booklet.

What is included in the kitchen?

The kitchen is fully equipped with a family-sized fridge with freezer, a two-burner electric stove, full sets of plates, glasses, and utensils, along with enough pots and pans for your cooking needs.

Location

How far is the nearest airport?

The nearest airport is Simón Bolívar International Airport (Santa Marta Airport), located less than 15 minutes away by car. A taxi from the airport to our suite typically costs around $35,000 COP (approximately $10 USD).

What activities are available nearby?

Bello Horizonte Beach is a 2-minute walk away, perfect for swimming and sunbathing. Zazué Plaza is only 5 minutes away, offering a variety of shops and restaurants.

On what building are we located?

Our suite is located in the Porto Horizonte building, situated in the same exclusive area as the Hilton, Mercure, and Irotama hotels.

What is the nearest grocery store?

Zazué Plaza is 5 minutes away and offers grocery shopping options. There is also a small market just 2 blocks away, and several restaurants within 2 blocks of the building.
