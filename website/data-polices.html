Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information.

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Sierra & Sea Suite collects, uses, stores, and protects the personal data of users who interact with our website and booking platforms. We are committed to complying with applicable data protection regulations, including Colombian law, U.S. regulations, and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information Collected

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number through our contact forms. We do not collect financial information directly, as third-party platforms like Airbnb and VRBO manage all bookings and payment processing independently.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Your personal data is used for two primary purposes: managing booking communications and rental inquiries, and sharing promotional content about Sierra & Sea Suite properties with your consent.

4. Use of Cookies

We use cookies on our website for Google Analytics and Google Ads to analyze traffic and enhance our marketing efforts. When you visit our website, you may accept or reject cookies upon entry.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information, as well as restrict its usage. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@sierraseasuite.com.

6. Process for Exercising Rights

To request the deletion, modification, or updating of your personal data, please send an email to info@sierraseasuite.com. We will respond to your request in a timely manner.

7. International Data Transfer

Your data may be transferred to servers located in the United States or other locations where Webflow or our booking platforms operate. We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of such transfers.

8. Data Security

Your information is stored on booking platforms and Webflow. We do not share your data with third parties except when required by law.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@sierraseasuite.com.
