Kitchen and Cooktop (Translated)

To start cooking on the induction cooktop:

  • Press the Power control to turn on the cooktop.
  • Place a suitable pot on the selected cooking zone. Make sure both the pot base and the cooktop surface are clean and dry.
  • Select the cooking zone that corresponds to where the pot is placed.
  • Adjust heat level with the slider: move toward + to increase power, toward - to decrease power.
  • If no heat level is selected within around 1 minute, the cooktop may turn off automatically. Start again from step 1.

If a burner does not respond, verify the pot is induction-compatible and centered on the selected zone.
