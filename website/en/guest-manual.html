Building Rules and Recommendations

  • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM.
  • Parties are not allowed.
  • Smoke-free space (no smoking in the apartment/building).
  • No pets allowed.
  • Please follow building rules and environmental care guidance.
  • Manage trash properly according to building procedures.
  • Beach/sand towel guidance: avoid taking unit towels outside and avoid bringing sand back into the apartment.

Extra reminders from the manual

  • The stove/cooktop heats quickly; adjust temperature carefully and use a heat pad/trivet.
  • Use the air conditioning with windows closed.

Check-out

  • Please leave dishes clean before departure.

Standard stay details remain: check-in at 3:00 PM and check-out at 11:00 AM unless your reservation says otherwise.
