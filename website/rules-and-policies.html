Rental Rules and Policies

Please review the following rules and policies to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for all guests.

1. Check-in and Check-out

  • Check-in Time: 3:00 PM
  • Check-out Time: 11:00 AM
  • Identification and required paperwork must be submitted before arrival.
  • Wristbands will be provided upon arrival for all registered guests.

Special timing requests should be made in advance for possible accommodation.

2. Guest Access and Registration

  • Only guests listed on the reservation and who have provided identification in advance are allowed.
  • Building wristbands are required for all guests during their stay.

3. Noise and Quiet Hours

  • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM.
  • No loud gatherings or parties are allowed.

4. No Smoking or Pets

  • Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the unit and the building.
  • Designated outdoor smoking areas are available.
  • No pets are permitted on the property.

5. Pool and Common Areas

  • Three pools are available, including one designed for children.
  • Fully equipped gym available for guest use.
  • Sauna is currently out of service.

6. Child Safety

  • The apartment is located on the 17th floor; parents must supervise children at all times.
  • Children must be supervised in pool and gym areas.
  • A playard is available for babies.

7. Minimum Stay and Occupancy

  • Minimum reservation: 2 nights.
  • Maximum occupancy: 5 guests and 1 baby.
  • Bedding: 1 Queen Bed, 1 Queen Sofa Bed, 1 Small Sofa Bed, 1 Playard.

8. Legal Compliance

  • A signed rental contract is required for all reservations.
  • Minors are only permitted to stay with their parents or legal guardians.
  • Valid identification is required for all guests at check-in.

9. No Parties or Events

  • The apartment is for residential use only, and no parties or large gatherings are allowed.

10. Damages and Liability

  • Guests are responsible for any damage costs incurred during their stay.

11. Parking

  • Limited secure on-site parking is available.

12. Laundry Service

  • No on-site laundry is available; local pick-up and delivery services can be arranged.

13. Internet and Entertainment

  • High-speed Wi-Fi is included.
  • Smart TVs with Netflix and streaming apps are available.

14. Emergency Contact Information

In case of emergency, contact the front desk or apartment manager via the information provided in the apartment booklet.
